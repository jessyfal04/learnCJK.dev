learnCJK.dev

Minimal tools to explore CJK characters

Learn and explore Chinese/Japanese/Korean characters:

  • Character lookup: forms, composition, and variants.
  • Lists: RTK/RTH/RSH and Hanja lists with ordering and details.

Quick API

GET /api/char?char=漢
GET /api/lists?type=rtk&field=chars
GET /api/lists?type=hanja&field=fields
